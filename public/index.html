INTRODUCTION

The Efs Academy started its journey in 2009. The management of The Academy, in keeping with their commitment to imparting quality education, wanted to bring this excellence to the elementary and high secondary levels.

In this short time period, The Efs Academy has grown phenomenally, with fourteen well-established campuses across the country, The Efs Academy has lived up to its expectations of differentiating itself and provides education par-excellence.

The Academy’s standards remain impeccable and the focus on learning beyond classrooms makes it stand out from other contemporary ooling systems.

The EFs Academy is a purpose built project, recognized for its learner centered classroom settings embedded in a modern learning backdrop. This holistic education base cultivates the moral, psychological and physical dimensions of the learners, facilitating them in expressing and reinventing themselves. The Academy fosters on a global humanistic identity, vital in today’s rapidly transforming socio-cultural world.Within half a decade, The Academy has succeeded in creating a learning subset that goes beyond conventional teaching, instituting continuous professional development programs, parallel workshops for parents, and outreach operations through ‘institutionalizing’ contacts.

The Academy continue to pursue higher standards, with an emphasis on fostering independence and creativity of the learner, while consistently endeavoring to transform and adapt new initiatives of academic distinction.



STRATEGY & APPROACH

At The Efs Academy, we believe in excellence in academics. We practice a learner-centered approach to teaching, with a proactive methodology of innovative instruction that fosters a meaningful educational experience.

We have trained faculty members who provide individualized learning experience in partnership with parents.


While following these overreaching aims, we never lose the sight of making sure our students add value to society, become successful human beings, and contribute toward making this world a better place to live.
